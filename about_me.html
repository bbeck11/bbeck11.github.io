About Me

Rock2Mars has played Overwatch since 2017 and has four years of team coaching experience. He has head-coached teams for the SVB tournaments and in the Overwatch Tranquility League. The Dreamboats, Sky Guardians, and Jet Pack Cats to name a few. Rock2Mars specializes in the support role and peaked in the top 500 of supports in season 35 of Overwatch 1. He has helped new players learn the fundamentals of Overwatch and improve the decision-making of diamond-plus players.
