Team Coaching

  • As a team coach, I will assess the strengths and weaknesses of your players and suggest a team comp that caters to their strengths.

These are the main things that create a successful team.

Private Coaching

  • For Individual coaching, I will assess the player's FPS (first-person shooter) skills and general hero competence.

    These sessions will focus on training routines for hero skill proficiency and the decision-making process of how to play based on which heroes are in the match.
